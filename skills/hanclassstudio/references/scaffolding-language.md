# Scaffolding Language

HanClassStudio teaches Chinese as the target language.

The teacher may choose one scaffolding language, such as English, Arabic, Russian, Thai, Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, or Indonesian.

Use the scaffolding language only to support comprehension, instructions, hints, and feedback. It must not replace Chinese input, Chinese output, or the core classroom task.

Keep learner-facing practice centered on Chinese words, sentences, listening, speaking, reading, and interaction.
